CyberEye - Founding Implementation and Tech Partner for the TSPCC

MoU exchange between CyberEye and TSPCC (left to right in picture, Shri Kalmeshwar Shingenavar, DCP-Crime, Cyberabad, Ram Ganesh, CEO, CyberEye, Shri Stephen Raveendra, CP Cyberabad, Shri Krishna Yedula, General Secretary, SCSC)

Telangana State Police Center of Excellence for Cyber Safety (TSPCC) is an initiative of the Telangana State Police to create a safe cyber ecosystem with an industry-led approach. CyberEye has been the Founding Implementation and Tech Partner for the TSPCC (Centre of Excellence). CyberEye played a fundamental role in bringing the initiative from conceptualization to operationalization.


The team is working closely with the TSPCC to design and implement solutions that would enable police departments to undertake digital transformation initiatives.
