Career Options after LL.B in India

Law as a career, has been gaining increasing significance over the years, especially in India. With reportedly over sixty thousand graduates every year, it has become important for one to carve a niche in a specified field of law so as to increase one’s degree of success early on in their career. Here are some of the many options to pursue after the completion of a law OCEAN…..

  3. Company Secretary
  4. Contract Analyst
  5. Civil Services (UPSC/SPSC)
  6. Judicial Services
  7. Administrative Services Officer
  8. PSU Recruitment
  9. Academician
  10. Legal Researcher
  11. Legal Content Writer
  12. Legal Journalist
  13. Policy Making and Legislative Drafting
  14. Dispute Resolution Professional (Arbitration/Mediation/Conciliation/Negotiation)
  15. JAG Officer
